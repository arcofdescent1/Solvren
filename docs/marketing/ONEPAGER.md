# Solvren — Product One-Pager

**Governance for Revenue-Impacting Changes**

---

## What It Is

Solvren is a **Revenue Change Governance Platform** that governs revenue-impacting system changes through structured intake, automated coordination, and predictive risk analysis.

Replace ad-hoc coordination across Slack, spreadsheets, and Jira with a systematic, auditable process.

---

## Key Capabilities

| Capability | What It Does |
|------------|--------------|
| **Structured Intake** | Guided capture of systems, domains, and impact. One place to describe the change. |
| **Coordination Autopilot** | Automatically suggests the right approvers and evidence based on governance rules. |
| **Revenue Impact Reports** | AI-generated risk analysis: failure modes, safeguards, required approvals. |
| **Approval Governance** | Domain- and system-based routing. Finance changes → Finance Reviewer. Stripe changes → Billing Owner. |
| **Visibility Dashboards** | Executives see risk exposure, blocked items, and overdue changes in real time. |

---

## Example Use Cases

- **Pricing changes** — Stripe, Chargebee pricing logic updates. Finance + Billing Owner review.
- **Billing logic** — Invoice generation, subscription lifecycle. Evidence and approval traceability.
- **Revenue recognition** — NetSuite, ERP rule changes. Compliance and audit trail.
- **Integration changes** — Webhooks, API, CRM sync. Multi-system coordination and risk visibility.
- **Schema / data changes** — CRM fields, data migration. Data integrity and stakeholder clarity.

---

## Benefits

- **Reduce revenue risk** — Changes analyzed and reviewed before they ship. Evidence enforced.
- **Faster coordination** — No more "who approves this?" Approvers and evidence suggested automatically.
- **Clear visibility** — Executives see what's in flight, what's blocked, where risk is.
- **Institutional memory** — Audit trail for every change. Lessons captured, not lost.
