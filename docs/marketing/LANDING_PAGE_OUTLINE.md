# Solvren — Landing Page Outline

Outline for website landing page. Sections and copy structure.

---

## 1. Headline

**Solvren — Governance for Revenue-Impacting Changes**

*Subheadline:* Structured intake. Automated coordination. Risk visibility.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

**Section:** The Problem

**Headline:** Revenue system changes are high risk. Most coordination is ad hoc.

**Body:**

- Changes to billing, pricing, and revenue systems carry real financial risk
- Coordination happens across Slack threads, Jira tickets, and spreadsheets
- Approvals are unclear. Evidence is inconsistent.
- Risk is invisible until something breaks.
- No audit trail when it does.

---

## 3. Key Capabilities

**Section:** What Solvren Does

**Headline:** One system of record for revenue-impacting changes.

**Three columns (or cards):**

| Risk Analysis | Automated Coordination | Governance Visibility |
|---------------|------------------------|------------------------|
| AI-generated Revenue Impact Reports: failure modes, safeguards, recommended approvals | Coordination Autopilot suggests approvers and evidence from governance rules | Executive dashboards: risk exposure, blocked items, overdue changes |
| Structured intake captures systems, domains, and impact | One click applies suggestions. No more "who do I loop in?" | Full audit trail for compliance and post-incident review |

---

## 4. Example Workflow

**Section:** How It Works

**Headline:** From change to approval in one place.

**Steps:**

1. **Create change** — Guided intake: systems, domain, change type
2. **Generate plan** — Coordination Autopilot suggests approvers and evidence
3. **Generate report** — Revenue Impact Report analyzes risk and safeguards
4. **Submit** — Reviewers see full context before approving
5. **Visibility** — Executives see status, risk, and audit trail
