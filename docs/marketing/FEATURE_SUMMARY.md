# Solvren — Feature Summary Sheet

Short bullet summary of product capabilities. Use for sales, beta recruitment, and internal alignment.

---

## Core Features

### Structured Change Intake

- Guided step-by-step capture of systems, domains, and impact
- Change type, rollout strategy, customer impact
- Revenue impact areas and risk signals
- Saves progress; supports drafts and partial completion

### Coordination Autopilot

- Automatically suggests approvers from domain, system, and change type mappings
- Suggests evidence (PR, test plan, rollback plan, etc.) based on risk level
- One-click apply approvers and evidence
- Coordination plan marked stale when change or mappings change

### Revenue Impact Analysis

- AI-generated Revenue Impact Reports per change
- Risk level (Low / Medium / High / Very High / Critical)
- Failure modes with severity and likelihood
- Required and recommended safeguards
- Executive summary: why it matters, worst outcome, what reduces risk

### Approval Governance

- Domain- and system-based approver routing
- Approval roles and mappings configurable per org
- Evidence requirements by risk bucket
- Approval blocks when required evidence missing

### Dashboards and Search

- Dashboard: My Approvals, In Review, Blocked, Overdue
- Executive view: Revenue at Risk, critical change counts, top risk drivers
- Full-text search: changes, systems, approvals, evidence, users
- Grouped results, filters, keyboard-friendly quick search

### RBAC and Restricted Visibility

- Roles: Owner, Admin, Submitter, Reviewer, Viewer
- Domain permissions: view and review by domain
- Restricted changes visible only to explicitly granted users
- Org-boundary enforcement; no cross-org leakage

---

## Summary Bullets (Elevator Pitch)

- **Structured intake** — Guided capture of systems, domains, and impact
- **Coordination autopilot** — Automatic approver and evidence suggestions
- **Revenue impact analysis** — AI-generated risk reports with failure modes and safeguards
- **Approval governance** — Domain/system routing, evidence enforcement
- **Dashboards and search** — Executive visibility, fast retrieval
- **RBAC and restricted visibility** — Role-based access, restricted changes
