# Executive Proof Packet

The Solvren proof packet is the board-ready evidence package for a revenue-impacting change or risk.

It should answer:

- What revenue is exposed?
- What decision is needed?
- What evidence exists?
- Which systems or customers are affected?
- What is the recommended action?
- What happened after the action?
- What value was protected, recovered, or accelerated?

Current change packets are available from change detail pages and the approval packet API in Markdown and PDF formats. The app should keep the packet plain-English first, with system details available for audit and engineering review.
