## 1. Product Positioning and Category

### Category

**Revenue Change Governance Platform**

Solvren sits at the intersection of change management, governance, and operational risk for revenue-impacting systems. It is not a generic ticketing tool or workflow engine—it is purpose-built for changes that affect billing, pricing, revenue recognition, and revenue systems.

### Core Positioning Statement

> **Solvren** governs revenue-impacting system changes through structured intake, automated coordination, and predictive risk analysis.

It replaces ad-hoc governance across Slack, spreadsheets, Jira tickets, and tribal knowledge with a systematic, auditable process.

### Problem Statement

Most companies manage revenue system changes through:

- **Informal coordination** — Slack threads and ad-hoc approvals
- **Fragmented tools** — Jira for tickets, spreadsheets for sign-offs, docs for policies
- **Undocumented knowledge** — "Ask Sarah" or "Finance always reviews Stripe"

This creates:

- **Revenue incidents** — Billing errors, double charges, incorrect recognition
- **Operational delays** — Unclear ownership, slow approvals
- **Unclear accountability** — No audit trail when something goes wrong

Solvren solves this by providing:

- **Structured intake** — Guided capture of systems, domains, and impact
- **Automated coordination** — System suggests approvers and evidence
- **Approval governance** — Domain- and system-based routing with audit trail
- **Risk analysis** — AI-generated failure modes and safeguards
- **Visibility dashboards** — Executive view of risk exposure and operational status

### Key Product Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Revenue Impact Intelligence** | AI-generated risk analysis for revenue-impacting changes. Failure modes, safeguards, and recommended approvals—all in a structured, executive-ready format. |
| **Coordination Autopilot** | Automatic assignment of reviewers and safeguards based on domain, system, and change type. Reduces manual coordination and ensures consistency. |
| **Governance Visibility** | Dashboards showing risk exposure, approval status, blocked items, and overdue changes. Executives see what matters without digging. |

### Competitive Comparison Narrative

Most organizations rely on:

- **Jira** for tickets
- **Slack** for coordination
- **Spreadsheets** for approvals
- **Documentation** for policies

Solvren integrates these into a **single system of record** for revenue-impacting changes. One place to create, review, approve, and audit.

## 2. Executive Value Narrative

### Executive Outcomes

Executives buy outcomes, not features. Solvren delivers:

| Outcome | What It Means |
|---------|---------------|
| **Reduced revenue risk** | Changes are analyzed and reviewed before they ship. Evidence and approvals are enforced. |
| **Faster change coordination** | Approvers and evidence are suggested automatically. Less back-and-forth. |
| **Clear governance visibility** | Executives see which changes are in flight, blocked, or overdue, and where revenue is at risk. |
| **Institutional memory** | Every change leaves an audit trail. Lessons and approvals are captured, not lost. |

### Executive Messaging

**Without Solvren**

> Revenue system changes are coordinated through Slack and tickets. Approvals are unclear. Evidence is inconsistent. Risk is invisible until something breaks.

**With Solvren**

> Every change goes through structured intake. The system predicts risk and suggests safeguards. Approvers and evidence are automatically coordinated. Executives see risk and operational status in real time.

### Executive Summary (Example)

> Solvren ensures every revenue-impacting system change is reviewed, documented, and coordinated before it ships.
>
> The platform analyzes risk, assigns the correct stakeholders automatically, and provides executives visibility into potential revenue exposure across active changes.

### Executive Benefits

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Risk reduction** | Prevent billing or revenue recognition errors through structured review and evidence. |
| **Faster coordination** | Reduce manual coordination overhead with automated approver and evidence suggestions. |
| **Governance compliance** | Maintain approval traceability and audit trail for compliance and post-incident review. |
| **Institutional knowledge** | Capture operational lessons, evidence, and approvals in a searchable system of record. |
