## Overview

Solvren supports integrations with revenue-related systems. Each integration produces signals that detectors consume.

---

## Integration Types

| Category | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Billing** | Subscriptions, invoices, usage, payment events |
| **CRM** | Accounts, opportunities, revenue attribution |
| **Pricing** | Rules, discounts, entitlements |
| **Other** | Per deployment; contact your admin |

---

## Connecting

Navigate to the Integrations area in admin or platform settings. Select an integration, authenticate, and authorize Solvren to read the required data. See [Connect Your First System](/docs/get-started/connect-your-first-system).
