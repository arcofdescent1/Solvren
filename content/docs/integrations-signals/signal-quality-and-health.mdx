## Overview

Healthy signals are essential for accurate detection. Solvren monitors signal quality and integration health so you can identify and fix pipeline issues.

---

## What to Monitor

- **Ingestion rate** — Are signals flowing as expected?
- **Latency** — How long from source to Solvren?
- **Completeness** — Are required fields present?
- **Errors** — Failed syncs or auth issues

---

## Integration Health

The Admin & Governance area includes integration health dashboards. Use them to:

- See connection status per integration
- Identify stale or failing syncs
- Troubleshoot authentication problems

See [Integration Health](/docs/admin-governance/integration-health).
