## Overview

The **Policy Center** is where admins configure approval gates, automation rules, and safe mode settings.

---

## What You Configure

- **Approval requirements** — Who must approve what
- **Confidence thresholds** — When to auto-execute vs require approval
- **Impact thresholds** — Require approval above certain impact levels
- **Role mappings** — Map approvers to playbooks or issue types
