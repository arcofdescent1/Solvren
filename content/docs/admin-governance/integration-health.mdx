## Overview

Integration health dashboards show connection status, signal flow, and any issues with your connected systems.

---

## What to Monitor

- **Connection status** — Green/red per integration
- **Last sync** — When data was last received
- **Error rate** — Failed syncs or auth issues
- **Signal volume** — Expected vs actual

---

## Troubleshooting

When health is degraded:
- Check authentication and tokens
- Verify API quotas and rate limits
- Review [Signal Quality and Health](/docs/integrations-signals/signal-quality-and-health)
