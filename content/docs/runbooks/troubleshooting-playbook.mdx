## Change Stuck in Review

Possible causes:

• missing approvals  
• incomplete evidence  
• reviewer unavailable

Resolution steps:

1. Check approval status
2. Verify evidence checklist
3. notify pending reviewers

---

## Missing Evidence

If evidence requirements are not satisfied:

1. review required evidence types
2. upload missing documentation
3. update change submission

---

## Incorrect Approval Routing

If incorrect reviewers appear:

1. verify approval role mappings
2. check domain configuration
3. update governance policies
