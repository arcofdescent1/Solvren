## Overview

Playbook performance metrics help you understand which playbooks drive the most value and where to optimize.

---

## Metrics

- **Value attributed** — Recovered + avoided per playbook
- **Resolution rate** — Issues resolved vs total
- **Time to resolution** — How quickly issues are addressed
- **False positive rate** — Issues that didn't need action

---

## Optimization

- Enable high-value playbooks first
- Tune confidence thresholds to balance automation and accuracy
- Use simulations to validate before enabling
