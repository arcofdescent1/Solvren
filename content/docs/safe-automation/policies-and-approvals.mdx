## Overview

**Policies** define who must approve actions and under what conditions. Approval gates ensure high-risk or low-confidence actions get human review.

---

## Policy Types

| Type | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Approval required** | Action needs explicit approval before execution |
| **Confidence threshold** | Auto-approve above a confidence level |
| **Impact threshold** | Require approval above an impact amount |
| **Role-based** | Certain roles must approve |
