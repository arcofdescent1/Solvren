## Overview

A strong organization setup ensures that Coordination Autopilot, approvals, evidence, and visibility all work correctly from the start.

## What to configure first

<Steps>
  <Step number={1} title="Create the organization foundation">
    Confirm organization name, timezone, notification settings, and review SLA defaults.
  </Step>
  <Step number={2} title="Invite the core team">
    Add owners, admins, submitters, reviewers, and viewers.
  </Step>
  <Step number={3} title="Register revenue-impacting systems">
    Add systems like Stripe, Chargebee, HubSpot, Salesforce, and NetSuite.
  </Step>
  <Step number={4} title="Create approval roles and mappings">
    Define who owns Finance, Security, Billing, Data, and other governance areas.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Recommended initial roles

Typical roles include Owner, Admin, Submitter, Reviewer, and Viewer.

## Recommended initial approval roles

Examples: Finance Reviewer, Security Reviewer, Billing Owner, Revenue Leadership, Data Reviewer.
