## Overview

Notification Configuration controls how users receive alerts about changes that require their attention.

Notifications ensure that governance workflows progress efficiently.

Administrators can configure:

• notification channels  
• alert rules  
• delivery preferences

---

## Notification Channels

Solvren supports several notification channels.

---

### Email Notifications

Email alerts notify users when:

• approvals are requested  
• evidence is missing  
• reviewers leave comments  
• changes become overdue

Email notifications ensure that important events are not missed.

---

### Slack Notifications

Slack notifications deliver alerts directly to team communication channels.

Common Slack alerts include:

• approval requests  
• completed approvals  
• change submissions

Slack integration helps teams coordinate quickly.

---

### In-App Notifications

In-app alerts appear within the Solvren interface.

These alerts provide immediate visibility when users log in.

---

## Configuring Notifications

Administrators can configure notification settings by:

1. Navigating to **Admin → Notifications**
2. Selecting the notification channel
3. Configuring delivery preferences

These settings apply to all users within the organization.

---

## Recommended Configuration

Organizations typically configure notifications to ensure that:

• reviewers receive approval alerts immediately  
• operational teams receive deployment alerts  
• overdue approvals trigger reminders

Proper notification settings help maintain efficient governance workflows.
