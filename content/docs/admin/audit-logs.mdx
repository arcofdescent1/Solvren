## Overview

Audit Logs record all significant actions performed within Solvren.

These logs provide a complete history of governance activity.

Audit logs support:

• operational accountability  
• compliance requirements  
• incident investigations

Administrators can review these records to understand how decisions were made.

---

## Logged Events

Solvren records events such as:

• change creation  
• evidence updates  
• approval decisions  
• comment activity  
• configuration changes

Each log entry includes:

• timestamp  
• user identity  
• action performed  
• associated change

---

## Reviewing Audit Logs

Administrators can review logs by:

1. Navigating to **Admin → Audit Logs**
2. Filtering events by user, change, or date
3. Reviewing historical actions

This allows administrators to investigate operational activity.

---

## Why Audit Logs Matter

Audit logs provide transparency into governance workflows.

Organizations use audit logs to:

• review operational decisions  
• investigate incidents  
• verify compliance policies

These records ensure that operational changes remain traceable.

---

## Retention Policies

Organizations may configure retention policies for audit logs depending on internal governance requirements.

Some organizations retain audit records for several years for compliance purposes.
