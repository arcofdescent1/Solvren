## What approval mappings do

Approval mappings power Coordination Autopilot.

They answer:

- who should review Finance changes
- who should review Stripe changes
- who should review Pricing changes

## Common mapping examples

- Domain: Finance → Finance Reviewer
- System: Stripe → Billing Owner
- Change Type: Pricing → Revenue Leadership

## Why this matters

Without mappings, coordination remains manual.

With mappings, the system can automatically suggest the correct reviewers when a change is created or updated.

## Recommended mapping strategy

Start with:

1. domain mappings
2. critical system mappings
3. high-risk change type mappings

Then expand after the first few live workflows.

## Validation tips

Before relying on mappings in production-like workflows, confirm:

- each mapped role has members
- the members actually have domain review permission where required
- your most common change types resolve to the expected reviewers
