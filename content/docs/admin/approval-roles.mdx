## What approval roles are

Approval roles are reusable groups for governance responsibility. Examples: Finance Reviewer, Billing Owner, Security Reviewer, Revenue Leadership, Data Reviewer.

## Why approval roles matter

Roles make governance more stable than assigning individuals. Define that Stripe changes go to Billing Owner, then manage Billing Owner membership separately.

## How approval roles are used

Approval roles feed into approval mappings, Coordination Autopilot, reviewer suggestions, and coverage checks.

## Best practice

Use approval roles for responsibilities that outlive any single individual.
