## What is Solvren?

Solvren is a revenue protection platform that detects issues, automates resolution, and proves ROI.

---

## The Closed-Loop System

Solvren operates as a continuous loop:

**Detect → Quantify → Act → Verify → Prove**

---

## Core Capabilities

### Signal Ingestion

Connect systems and ingest operational and financial data.

### Detection

Identify issues using detector packs.

### Impact Quantification

Prioritize issues based on financial impact.

### Execution

Run playbooks to resolve issues.

### Verification

Confirm outcomes using real system data.

### ROI Tracking

Measure recovered and protected revenue.

---

## Why It Matters

Most systems stop at detection.

Solvren ensures issues are:

- Found  
- Fixed  
- Verified  
- Measured
