## Signal

A raw event from an integrated system.

---

## Detector

Logic that identifies patterns or anomalies.

---

## Issue

A detected problem impacting revenue.

---

## Impact

Estimated financial effect of an issue.

---

## Playbook

A defined set of actions to resolve an issue.

---

## Execution

The process of applying a playbook.

---

## Verification

Confirmation that an issue has been resolved.

---

## Value

Measured outcome, including recovered revenue or avoided loss.

---

## Safe Automation

Controlled execution using policies and approvals.
