## End-to-End Flow

Solvren processes data through a clear pipeline:

### 1. Integrations & Signals

You connect revenue systems (billing, CRM, pricing, etc.). Solvren ingests data as **signals**. Signal quality and health are monitored.

### 2. Detectors & Issues

**Detector packs** analyze signals. When they find potential problems, they create **issues** with impact estimates and confidence scores.

### 3. Impact & Prioritization

The impact engine quantifies revenue at risk. Issues are prioritized by impact, confidence, and policy rules.

### 4. Actions & Playbooks

**Playbooks** define what to do for each issue type. They can recommend actions, simulate fixes, or (with policy approval) execute them via write-back.

### 5. Verification & ROI

When actions run, Solvren verifies outcomes and attributes **recovered revenue** or **avoided loss**. The ROI dashboard surfaces this to executives.

### 6. Safe Automation

**Policies** control who approves what. **Safe mode** and confidence thresholds govern when automation can run. **Simulations** allow testing without execution.
