## What types of changes should be tracked?

Solvren is designed for **revenue-impacting operational changes**.

Examples include:

• pricing rule updates  
• billing configuration changes  
• CRM routing changes  
• revenue recognition updates

---

## Do all operational changes need approval?

Not necessarily.

Organizations can configure which domains require formal approvals.

High-risk domains such as billing or pricing typically require multiple reviewers.

---

## Can approvals be customized?

Yes.

Administrators can configure:

• approval roles  
• approval mappings  
• domain-specific requirements

---

## Can we integrate with Slack?

Yes.

Solvren can deliver notifications and approval alerts through Slack.

---

## Why is evidence required?

Evidence ensures that operational safeguards exist before a change is implemented.

This reduces operational risk and improves incident response readiness.
