## Overview

Solvren notifies users when action is required.

Notifications ensure that approvals and coordination tasks are not missed.

---

# Notification Types

Users may receive notifications for:

• approval requests  
• missing evidence  
• reviewer comments  
• completed approvals  
• overdue changes

---

# Notification Channels

Notifications may be delivered through:

### Email

Email alerts notify users when they must review or approve changes.

---

### Slack

Slack notifications provide real-time updates within team collaboration channels.

---

### In-App Alerts

In-app notifications appear within the Solvren interface.

These alerts provide quick visibility when users log in.

---

# Managing Notifications

Users can configure notification preferences in their account settings.

Administrators may configure organization-wide defaults.

---

# Best Practices

Teams should ensure:

• reviewers receive approval alerts  
• operational teams receive deployment alerts  
• high-risk domains trigger immediate notifications
