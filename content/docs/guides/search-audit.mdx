## Overview

Solvren provides powerful search and audit capabilities.

These features allow teams to quickly locate historical decisions and investigate past changes.

---

# Search

Users can search across:

• change titles  
• systems  
• domains  
• reviewers  

Search helps teams answer questions such as:

• "Who approved the pricing change last quarter?"  
• "When was billing logic modified?"  

---

# Audit History

Every action in the system is recorded.

Tracked actions include:

• change creation  
• evidence updates  
• approval decisions  
• configuration changes

---

# Why Audit Logs Matter

Audit logs support:

• operational accountability  
• compliance requirements  
• incident investigations

Organizations can trace exactly who approved or modified a change.
