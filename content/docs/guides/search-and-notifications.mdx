## Search

Solvren search helps users quickly find changes, systems, approvals, evidence, and users.

## What to search by

Useful search terms include change title, system name, domain, evidence type, and user name or email.

## Notifications

Notifications surface workflow events that require attention. Examples: approval requested, evidence required, approval overdue, change approved or rejected, comment or update notifications if configured.

## In-app notifications

Use the in-app notification surface to track product activity without leaving the application.

## Email and Slack routing

If configured, notifications can also be delivered by email or Slack.

## Best practice

Use search for retrieval and notifications for awareness. Search helps you find what exists. Notifications help you know what needs action.
