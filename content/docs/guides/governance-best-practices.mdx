## Overview

Effective governance requires thoughtful configuration of approvals and safeguards.

The following best practices help organizations minimize operational risk.

---

## Require Finance Review for Pricing Changes

Pricing updates can directly affect revenue and customer billing.

Recommended reviewers:

• Finance  
• Product  

---

## Require Engineering Review for System Changes

Changes that modify system logic should include technical review.

Recommended reviewers:

• Engineering  
• Platform team

---

## Enforce Evidence for High-Risk Domains

High-risk domains should require operational safeguards.

Examples:

| Domain | Required Evidence |
|------|------|
| Billing | rollback plan |
| Pricing | validation tests |
| Revenue recognition | financial reconciliation |

---

## Encourage Cross-Team Coordination

Operational changes often affect multiple departments.

Include reviewers from:

• product  
• engineering  
• finance  
• revenue operations

---

## Review Historical Changes

Audit history provides valuable insights.

Regularly review:

• rejected changes  
• incident-causing changes  
• frequently modified systems

This helps refine governance policies over time.
