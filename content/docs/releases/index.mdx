## Overview

Solvren has evolved from change governance to a full **revenue protection platform**. Release notes reflect this evolution: detectors, impact engine, policy center, execution reliability, verification/ROI, safe automation, and onboarding/playbook performance.

---

# Platform Evolution

### Detectors & Impact

- **Detector packs** — Analyze signals and create issues
- **Impact engine** — Quantify revenue at risk
- **Prioritization** — Impact and confidence drive ordering

### Policy & Safe Automation

- **Policy Center** — Configure approval and automation policies
- **Safe mode** — Confidence thresholds govern automation
- **Simulations** — Dry-run playbooks without execution

### Execution & Verification

- **Write-back** — Execute actions against external systems
- **Verification** — Attribute recovered revenue and avoided loss
- **ROI dashboard** — Executive visibility into value

### Onboarding & Performance

- **Path to first value** — Connect, detect, enable playbook, view value
- **Playbook performance** — Metrics and optimization guidance
