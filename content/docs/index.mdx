# Solvren Documentation

## Protect and grow your revenue with a closed-loop system

Solvren detects revenue-impacting issues, quantifies their impact, takes action, verifies outcomes, and proves ROI—automatically and safely.

---

## How Solvren Works

1. **Detect**  
   Continuously monitor your systems to identify issues across payments, CRM, and operations.

2. **Quantify**  
   Understand the real financial impact of each issue so you can prioritize effectively.

3. **Act**  
   Execute corrective actions using safe, reliable playbooks.

4. **Verify**  
   Confirm that actions succeeded and issues are resolved.

5. **Prove**  
   Track recovered revenue and avoided loss with a clear ROI.
