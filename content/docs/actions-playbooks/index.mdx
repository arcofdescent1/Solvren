## What are Playbooks?

Playbooks are structured workflows that resolve issues.

---

## Key Properties

- **Idempotent** — Safe to retry  
- **Observable** — Fully tracked  
- **Reliable** — State-managed execution  

---

## Execution Lifecycle

`pending` → `executing` → `success` | `failed` | `retrying`

(See [Writeback and Reliability](/docs/actions-playbooks/writeback-and-reliability) for retries, dead-letter, and integration health.)

---

## Reliability Features

- Retry orchestration  
- Error handling  
- External verification  
- Dead-letter handling  

---

## Example Playbooks

- Retry failed payment  
- Correct CRM record  
- Issue refund adjustment  

---

## Outcome

Playbooks ensure issues are resolved consistently and safely.
