## Overview

**Playbooks** map issue types to actions. When an issue is detected, the matching playbook determines what Solvren recommends or executes.

---

## Structure

A playbook typically includes:

- **Trigger** — Issue type or detector
- **Actions** — Steps to take (recommend, notify, simulate, execute)
- **Policy** — Approval requirements, safe mode settings

---

## Enabling Playbooks

Admins enable playbooks per environment. Policies (approvals, confidence thresholds) control automation level.
