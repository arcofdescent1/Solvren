## Overview

Solvren coordinates governance workflows across revenue systems.

The platform connects change management workflows with risk analysis and operational safeguards.

---

## Components

### Change Intake

Collects structured metadata about operational changes.

---

### Coordination Engine

Generates governance workflows including approvals and evidence requirements.

---

### Risk Analysis Engine

Analyzes potential financial risk associated with a change.

---

### Approval Workflows

Coordinate cross-team review and decision-making.

---

### Audit System

Records historical decisions and governance activity.
