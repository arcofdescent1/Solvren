## Overview

Solvren relies on background processing to keep operational workflows timely and visible. This typically includes notification dispatch, SLA evaluation, inbox generation, and digest generation.

## Why jobs matter

Without reliable background processing, users may not receive approvals, reminders, or queue updates at the right time.

## Notification model

Notifications may be delivered through in-app surfaces, email, and Slack.

## Operational requirements

Background jobs should be idempotent, observable, and retryable where appropriate.

## Why this matters to admins

Admins and owners should understand that the user experience depends not only on visible UI flows, but also on healthy background job execution.
