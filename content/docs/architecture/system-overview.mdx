## Overview

Solvren is a governance platform designed to manage operational changes that impact revenue systems.

The platform coordinates change workflows across multiple systems and teams.

---

# Core Components

The system consists of several major components.

---

## Change Management Engine

Tracks the lifecycle of operational changes.

Handles:

• intake workflows  
• lifecycle state transitions  
• coordination tracking

---

## Coordination Autopilot

Analyzes change metadata and recommends:

• required approvers  
• evidence requirements  
• operational safeguards

---

## Risk Analysis Engine

Evaluates the potential financial impact of a change.

Generates:

• risk scores  
• failure mode analysis  
• mitigation recommendations

---

## Audit and Compliance Layer

Tracks system actions and governance decisions.

Provides a complete historical record of operational changes.
