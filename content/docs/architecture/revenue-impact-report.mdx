## Purpose

The Revenue Impact Report helps teams understand the financial and operational risk of a change before it ships.

## What it includes

Typical report outputs include risk level, failure modes, impact areas, safeguards, and executive summary.

## Design model

The report combines deterministic baseline signals, structured report generation, persisted report versions, and stale detection after material edits.

## Why versioning matters

A report should reflect the current change state. If material fields change, the report may become stale and should be regenerated.
