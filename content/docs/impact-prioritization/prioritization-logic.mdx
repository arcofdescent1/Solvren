## Overview

Issues are prioritized so high-impact, high-confidence items surface first. The logic combines impact, confidence, age, and policy rules.

---

## Factors

| Factor | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Impact | Higher impact = higher priority |
| Confidence | Higher confidence = higher priority |
| Age | Older issues may be elevated |
| Policy | Custom rules can override default ordering |

---

## Ops Inbox

The ops inbox displays issues in priority order. Operators can filter and sort as needed.
