## Overview

The impact engine estimates revenue at risk for each issue. Models vary by issue type and available signal data.

---

## Model Types

- **Direct revenue** — Billing, subscription, or payment impact
- **Churn risk** — Customer or contract at risk
- **Attribution gaps** — Revenue misattributed or lost
- **Config drift** — Pricing or discount misconfigurations

---

## Inputs

Impact models use signal data, historical patterns, and configurable parameters. Results feed prioritization and policy decisions.
