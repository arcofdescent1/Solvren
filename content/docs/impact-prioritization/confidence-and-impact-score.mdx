## Overview

Each issue has an **impact** (revenue at risk) and a **confidence** (how certain the system is). Together they drive prioritization and safe automation.

---

## Confidence Levels

Higher confidence means the detector and impact model are more certain. Low confidence may require manual review before automation.

---

## Impact Score

Impact can be expressed as a dollar amount or severity band. It helps operators and playbooks decide urgency and which actions to take.

---

## Safe Automation

Policies often gate automation by confidence. High-confidence issues may be auto-resolved; low-confidence issues may require approval.
