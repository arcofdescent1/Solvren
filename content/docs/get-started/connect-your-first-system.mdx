## Overview

Before Solvren can detect issues, it needs data from your revenue systems. **Integrations** connect those systems and produce **signals** that detectors consume.

---

## What You'll Do

1. Navigate to the Integrations area in the admin or platform UI
2. Choose a supported integration (e.g., billing, CRM, pricing)
3. Authenticate and authorize Solvren to read the required data
4. Confirm that signals are flowing (check integration health)

---

## Supported Integrations

Solvren supports connections to:

- **Billing systems** — Subscriptions, invoices, usage
- **CRM platforms** — Accounts, opportunities, revenue attribution
- **Pricing engines** — Rules, discounts, entitlements
- **Other revenue-related systems** — Per your deployment

See [Supported Integrations](/docs/integrations-signals/supported-integrations) for the full list.

---

## After Connecting

Once signals are flowing:

- Detector packs can run against incoming data
- Issues will appear in the Issues inbox when detectors find problems
- You can move to [Detect Your First Issue](/docs/get-started/detect-your-first-issue)
