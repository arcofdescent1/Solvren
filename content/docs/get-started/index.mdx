## Welcome to Solvren

Solvren is a governance platform for **revenue-impacting operational changes**.

Modern revenue systems span many tools—billing systems, CRM platforms, marketing automation, pricing logic, and reporting infrastructure. Changes to any of these systems can introduce financial risk if they are not coordinated correctly.

Solvren helps teams:

• Identify revenue risk before deployment  
• Coordinate cross-functional approvals  
• Enforce operational safeguards  
• Document change decisions and evidence  
• Provide executive visibility into revenue risk

---

## How Solvren Works

Solvren introduces a structured workflow for managing high-impact operational changes.

The workflow consists of five phases:

1️⃣ **Draft the change**  
Define what is being modified and which systems are involved.

2️⃣ **Guided intake**  
Capture structured information about rollout plans, customer impact, and operational dependencies.

3️⃣ **Coordination Autopilot**  
Solvren suggests approvers, evidence requirements, and coordination tasks.

4️⃣ **Revenue Impact Analysis**  
Generate a structured report identifying failure modes and risk exposure.

5️⃣ **Approval and release**  
Submit the change for cross-functional review.

---

## Typical Changes Managed in Solvren

Examples include:

• Pricing or discount rule updates  
• Billing logic changes  
• CRM routing or lead assignment updates  
• Revenue recognition configuration changes  
• Contract structure changes  
• Payment processor configuration changes  
• Revenue reporting pipeline updates

## Steps to First Value

If you're new to Solvren, follow this path:

1️⃣ **Set up your organization**

Configure users, roles, and approval mappings.

2️⃣ **Create your first change**

Walk through the intake workflow and generate a risk report.

3️⃣ **Submit for review**

Experience the full approval workflow.
