## The fastest path to value

Solvren is designed to deliver value quickly. Most teams can detect and resolve their first revenue-impacting issue within minutes.

---

## Step 1: Connect your systems

Start by integrating your core systems such as Stripe, HubSpot, or Salesforce.

This enables Solvren to ingest signals and begin monitoring for issues.

**Guide:** [Connect your first system](/docs/get-started/connect-your-first-system)

---

## Step 2: Detect your first issue

Once connected, detector packs automatically identify issues such as:

- Failed payments  
- Data inconsistencies  
- Revenue mismatches  

Each issue includes supporting evidence and linked signals.

**Guide:** [Detect your first issue](/docs/get-started/detect-your-first-issue)

---

## Step 3: Run your first playbook

Select an issue and execute a playbook to resolve it.

Playbooks are:

- Safe  
- Idempotent  
- Retryable  
- Fully auditable  

**Guide:** [Enable your first playbook](/docs/get-started/enable-your-first-playbook)

---

## Step 4: Verify results

Solvren verifies that the action succeeded and confirms the issue is resolved.

---

## Step 5: See your first value

Track:

- Recovered revenue  
- Avoided loss  
- Impact over time  

**Guide:** [View your first value](/docs/get-started/view-your-first-value)

---

## What to expect next

Solvren will begin:

- Detecting new issues continuously  
- Recommending actions  
- Improving performance over time
