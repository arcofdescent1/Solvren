## Overview

**Detectors** analyze incoming signals and create **issues** when they find potential revenue impact. Your first detected issue is the starting point for the rest of the value loop.

---

## What You'll Do

1. Ensure at least one integration is connected and producing signals
2. Enable one or more **detector packs** for your environment
3. Wait for detectors to run (or trigger a run if supported)
4. Open the Issues inbox and inspect your first issue

---

## Understanding Issues

An issue typically includes:

- **Source** — Which detector and signal produced it
- **Impact** — Estimated revenue impact (if quantified)
- **Confidence** — How confident the system is in the finding
- **Recommended actions** — Playbooks that can address it

---

## After Detecting

Once you see an issue:

- Review its impact and confidence
- Decide whether to act manually or enable a playbook
- Proceed to [Enable Your First Playbook](/docs/get-started/enable-your-first-playbook) to automate resolution
