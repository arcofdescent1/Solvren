## Overview

**Detector packs** bundle detection logic for specific problem domains. They run against incoming signals and create issues when anomalies or risks are found.

---

## Example Pack Types

| Pack | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Billing anomalies | Duplicate charges, failed payments, subscription mismatches |
| Pricing drift | Rule changes, discount inconsistencies |
| CRM data quality | Missing fields, invalid mappings |
| Revenue attribution | Gaps in attribution logic |

(Exact packs vary by deployment.)

---

## Enabling Packs

Admins enable detector packs per environment. Once enabled, packs run on a schedule or in near real-time, depending on configuration.
