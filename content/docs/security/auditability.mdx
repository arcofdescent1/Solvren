## Why auditability matters

Solvren preserves the operational story of a change. That includes when it was created, when it moved into review, when evidence was added, who approved or rejected it, and when coordination or risk artifacts were generated.

## Timeline and audit history

Each change has a timeline that records major actions in order. Typical events include change created, change submitted, evidence provided, coordination plan generated, revenue impact report generated, approval approved or rejected.

## What auditability supports

A strong audit trail helps with incident analysis, governance reviews, operational handoffs, executive reporting, and compliance conversations.

<Callout variant="success" title="Design principle">
A good governance system should explain not just the final decision, but how the organization arrived there.
</Callout>
