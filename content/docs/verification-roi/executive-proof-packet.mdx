The executive proof packet summarizes a revenue-impacting change or risk in a format leaders can review outside the product.

It answers:

- What revenue is exposed?
- What decision is needed?
- What evidence exists?
- Which systems or customers are affected?
- What is the recommended action?
- What happened after the action?
- What value was protected, recovered, or accelerated?

Change packets are available from change detail pages in Markdown and PDF formats. The packet keeps plain-English business evidence first, with system details available for audit and engineering review.
