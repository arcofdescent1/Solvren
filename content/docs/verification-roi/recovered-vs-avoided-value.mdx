## Overview

Solvren attributes two types of value to actions: **recovered revenue** and **avoided loss**.

---

## Recovered Revenue

Value that was **lost** and **restored** by an action.

Examples:
- Billing error fixed → revenue collected
- Churned customer recovered
- Misapplied discount corrected

---

## Avoided Loss

Value that **would have been lost** if the issue had not been addressed.

Examples:
- Pricing drift caught before deployment
- Subscription at risk — intervention prevented churn
- Duplicate charge prevented

---

## Total ROI

Total ROI = Recovered revenue + Avoided loss. Both contribute to the value Solvren proves.
