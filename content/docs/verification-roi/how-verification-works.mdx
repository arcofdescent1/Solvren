## Overview

When an action runs, Solvren verifies the outcome and attributes value. Verification closes the loop from detection to proved ROI.

---

## Flow

1. Action executes (recommend, simulate, or write-back)
2. Outcome is checked (e.g., billing fix confirmed, churn prevented)
3. Value is attributed: recovered revenue or avoided loss
4. ROI dashboard and reports are updated

---

## Attribution

Value is attributed to the action and linked to the original issue. This enables tracking by playbook, detector, or time period.
