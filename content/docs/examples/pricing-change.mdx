## Overview

This example demonstrates how a pricing configuration change is managed through Solvren.

Pricing changes are high-impact because they directly affect customer billing and revenue generation.

---

## Scenario

An organization wants to introduce a new pricing tier for a product.

The change involves:

• updating pricing logic in the billing platform  
• updating pricing documentation  
• verifying invoice generation  

---

## Step 1 — Create the Change

Example change configuration:

| Field | Value |
|------|------|
| Title | Add new Pro pricing tier |
| Domain | Pricing |
| Systems | Stripe |
| Change Type | Pricing configuration |

---

## Step 2 — Coordination Autopilot

The system generates recommended governance steps.

Suggested approvals:

• Finance  
• Product

Suggested evidence:

• pricing rule validation tests  
• invoice simulation  
• rollback procedure

---

## Step 3 — Revenue Impact Report

The system identifies potential risks:

Failure modes:

• incorrect pricing applied to existing customers  
• invoice calculation errors  

Risk score: **Medium**

Recommended safeguards:

• test invoice generation  
• verify billing tier mapping

---

## Step 4 — Submit for Review

Approvers review:

• change description  
• risk report  
• evidence checklist

Finance verifies pricing logic before approval.

---

## Outcome

After approvals are complete, the change is approved and recorded in the audit history.

This example demonstrates how governance workflows reduce pricing configuration risk.
